CLINICAL PROGRESS NOTE

ASSESSMENT AND PLAN:
Assessment: Stage IV EGFR L858R-mutant adenocarcinoma. Notably, low-level pretreatment T790M detected, which has been reported in approximately 1-2% of treatment-naive EGFR-mutant NSCLC. This strongly favors first-line osimertinib over erlotinib. Plan: Start osimertinib 80 mg daily. Pericardiocentesis if hemodynamically significant.
