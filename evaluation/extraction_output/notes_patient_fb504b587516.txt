PATHOLOGY REPORT

DIAGNOSIS AND FINDINGS:
Colonoscopic biopsy of the descending colon mass showed moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma (histology 8140/3) with focal signet ring cell features.

---

GENOMIC PROFILING REPORT

RESULTS:
Comprehensive genomic profiling identified KRAS G12D mutation, BRAF wild-type, no PIK3CA mutation, TP53 R175H mutation, APC truncating mutation; tumor was microsatellite stable (MSS) with intact mismatch repair proteins.

---

RADIOLOGY REPORT

FINDINGS:

Please correlate clinically.

CLINICAL PROGRESS NOTE

ASSESSMENT AND PLAN:
