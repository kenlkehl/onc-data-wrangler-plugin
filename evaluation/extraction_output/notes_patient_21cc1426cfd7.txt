RADIOLOGY REPORT

PATHOLOGY REPORT

DIAGNOSIS AND FINDINGS:
At age 63, bronchoscopic biopsy with endobronchial ultrasound-guided transbronchial needle aspiration of right upper lobe lesion and station 11R lymph node. Right upper lobe: moderately differentiated squamous cell carcinoma. Station 11R: positive for metastatic squamous cell carcinoma. Immunohistochemistry: p40 positive, CK5/6 positive, TTF-1 negative. PD-L1 TPS 45% (22C3 assay).

GENOMIC PROFILING REPORT

At age 63, next-generation sequencing (Guardant360 CDx) of circulating tumor DNA: EGFR wild-type, ALK rearrangement not detected, ROS1 rearrangement not detected, TP53 R175H detected, CDKN2A p16 loss detected, NFE2L2 mutation detected. No actionable driver mutations. Microsatellite stable.

CLINICAL PROGRESS NOTE

ASSESSMENT AND PLAN:
At age 63, multidisciplinary tumor board discussion: stage IIIA (T3N1M0) squamous cell carcinoma with chest wall involvement. Potentially resectable with en bloc chest wall resection, but patient has limited pulmonary reserve (FEV1 1.8L, 55% predicted). Recommended neoadjuvant chemoimmunotherapy followed by surgical reassessment.
