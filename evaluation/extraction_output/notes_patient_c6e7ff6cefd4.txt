PATHOLOGY REPORT

DIAGNOSIS AND FINDINGS:
Rectosigmoid biopsy showed moderately differentiated adenocarcinoma (histology 8140/3) with prominent Crohn-like lymphoid reaction and no mucinous component.

---

GENOMIC PROFILING REPORT

RESULTS:

---

RADIOLOGY REPORT

FINDINGS:
CT chest/abdomen/pelvis at age 45 demonstrated rectosigmoid mass with bilateral pelvic sidewall lymphadenopathy and three hepatic metastases in the right lobe (largest 3.1 cm in segment V); no pulmonary metastases; staged T3N2bM1a.

Please correlate clinically.

CLINICAL PROGRESS NOTE

ASSESSMENT AND PLAN:
At age 45, baseline CEA was 64 ng/mL and CA 19-9 was 47 U/mL; despite KRAS G12D mutation, MSI-high status makes patient a candidate for first-line immunotherapy per KEYNOTE-177.
