PATHOLOGY REPORT

DIAGNOSIS AND FINDINGS:
At age 50, bronchoscopic biopsy of left upper lobe mass with EBUS-guided sampling of station 7 and 4L lymph nodes. Left upper lobe: poorly differentiated adenocarcinoma with signet ring cell features. Station 7 and 4L: positive for metastatic adenocarcinoma. Immunohistochemistry: TTF-1 positive, Napsin-A weakly positive, CK7 positive, CK20 negative, CDX2 negative. PD-L1 TPS 10% (22C3 assay).

GENOMIC PROFILING REPORT
