At age 73, initiated VRd induction with dose adjustments for renal impairment; bortezomib 1.3 mg/m2 subcutaneously weekly, lenalidomide 5 mg due to creatinine clearance of 22 mL/min, dexamethasone 40 mg weekly; dexamethasone also serving as treatment for hypercalcemia.
